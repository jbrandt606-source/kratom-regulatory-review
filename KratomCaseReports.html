This page provides an indexed compilation of published clinical case reports involving kratom exposure.

Reports are organized by primary body system: cardiovascular events, fatal toxicology findings, hepatic injury, neurologic complications, psychiatric effects, neonatal outcomes, and additional systemic toxicity.

Case reports describe individual clinical observations and do not establish incidence or causation. They are presented for structured review and reference in legislative and regulatory contexts.
