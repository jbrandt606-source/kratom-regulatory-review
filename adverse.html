How to Interpret These Datasets (Plain Language)
What these numbers mean

FAERS and CAERS are designed to help detect safety signals ‚Äî patterns that may warrant closer review. Reports can be submitted by health professionals, consumers, and manufacturers. A report may include multiple products or substances, and reporting intensity can change over time based on media attention, product labeling, and clinical awareness.

These datasets are useful for identifying what outcomes are being reported (e.g., hospitalization or death), but they do not establish how often an outcome occurs in the population and do not independently prove that kratom caused the outcome.
